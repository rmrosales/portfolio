UI / UX Design ⚡ 4 Months

The Project

Wanderpath is a platform designed for travel influencers and content creators, helping them discover destinations and manage their time effectively.

Developed with a user-focused approach, Wanderpath was shaped by continuous feedback and agile iterations. The result is a streamlined yet engaging experience that simplifies content planning.

Overview

It aims to help influencers and content creators plan and optimize their content creation process effectively. Without proper scheduling, they risk missing key shots—especially if they arrive at the wrong time.

By offering tailored guidance and insights, Wanderpath eliminates these challenges, ensuring a smoother and more efficient content creation experience.

How Might We Statement

How might we be able to simplify time management and destination selection for influencers and content creators new to a country or destination?

Content creators need to balance exploring new locations, learning about local culture, and finding spots that connect with their audience—all while managing tight schedules. It can be overwhelming with travel plans, the right lighting for photos or videos, and timing visits to match when their followers are most active.

The Problem

For influencers and content creators visiting a new place, managing time while researching and selecting content-worthy locations can be challenging.

They must balance exploring new spots, understanding local culture, and finding destinations that resonate with their audience—all while keeping up with tight content schedules. The process becomes even more overwhelming when factoring in travel logistics, optimal lighting conditions, and timing visits to maximize audience engagement.

The Solution

Wanderpath: A Smarter Way To Plan

Wanderpath streamlines the content creation process by providing influencers and content creators with curated recommendations based on location, time of day, and audience preferences. With intuitive planning tools, users can efficiently schedule visits, optimize lighting conditions, and align their content with peak engagement times. By reducing the guesswork, Wanderpath allows creators to focus on capturing the perfect shot while making the most of their time.

Key Takeaways

Prioritizing user needs and pain points led to the creation of features that enhance the experience of content discovery and planning.

Frequent testing and real user feedback helped refine the app’s functionality, ensuring it aligned with user expectations and needs.

Working on Wanderpath reinforced the importance of adaptability—balancing user needs, technical constraints, and business goals to create a product that truly delivers value.
