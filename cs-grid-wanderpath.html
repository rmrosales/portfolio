Overview

Wanderpath is a travel planning platform designed to help content creators and influencers craft unique, personalized travel experiences.

By leveraging AI and user preferences, it simplifies itinerary planning, allowing creators tofocus on capturing and sharing their journeys with their audience.

How Might We Statement

How might we be able to simplify time management and destination selection for influencers and content creators new to a country or destination?

Content creators need to balance exploring new locations, learning about local culture, and finding spots that connect with their audience—all while managing tight schedules. It can be overwhelming with travel plans, the right lighting for photos or videos, and timing visits to match when their followers are most active.

The Problem

Influencers and content creators new to a place often struggle to manage their time while researching and choosing destinations for their content.

This involves balancing the need to explore new locations, understand local culture, and identify spots that will resonate with their audience, all while adhering to tight content schedules. The process can be overwhelming, as they must also account for travel logistics, optimal lighting for photos or videos, and aligning their visits with peak engagement times for their followers.

“I went to Bali for a sunset shot, but it was too crowded. I'll need to return at dawn for the perfect shot.”

Jesse Trudeau

The Solution

Wanderpath aims to simplify time management and destination selection by offering personalized recommendations based on influencers’ content goals and audience engagement.

The platform helps users discover locations, consider factors like peak times, optimal lighting for photos, and local culture. With easy-to-follow tailored itineraries and real-time insights, Wanderpath takes the guesswork out of planning, so content creators can focus on capturing content.

Key Takeaways

Prioritizing user needs and pain points helped shape features that enhance the experience of travel content discovery and planning.

Prioritizing user needs led to intuitive features, while frequent testing reinforced the value of real user feedback.

Working on Wanderpath reinforced the importance of adaptability—balancing user needs, technical constraints, and business goals to create a product that truly delivers value.
