About

Wanderpath is a platform designed for travel influencers and content creators, helping them manage their time and discover destinations for their content.

It simplifies the process of planning and content creation, allowing users to easily find new locations and optimize their schedules for maximum efficiency.

Overview

Wanderpath is designed to help influencers and content creators efficiently plan and optimize their content creation process.

Without the right planning, they may miss opportunities to capture key shots, especially if they arrive at the wrong time.

Wanderpath aims to prevent these unexpected hurdles by providing tailored guidance and insights, promoting a more efficient content creation process for influencers and content creators.

“I visited the Great Wall but couldn't capture the shot I wanted because it was crowded with tourists when I arrived. I’ll need to return early in the morning the next day to get the perfect shot.”

Jesse Trudeau
Landscape Photographer

The Problem

Influencers and content creators new to a place often struggle to manage their time while researching and choosing destinations for their content.

This involves balancing the need to explore new locations, understand local culture, and identify spots that will resonate with their audience, all while adhering to tight content schedules. The process can be overwhelming, as they must also account for travel logistics, optimal lighting for photos or videos, and aligning their visits with peak engagement times for their followers.

“I went to Bali for a sunset shot, but it was too crowded. I'll need to return at dawn for the perfect shot.”

Jesse Trudeau
Landscape Photographer

How Might We Statement

How might we simplify time management and destination selection for influencers and content creators new to a country or destination?

Content creators need to balance exploring new locations, learning about local culture, and finding spots that connect with their audience—all while managing tight schedules. It can be overwhelming with travel plans, the right lighting for photos or videos, and timing visits to match when their followers are most active.

The Solution

Wanderpath simplifies time management and destination selection by offering personalized recommendations based on influencers’ content goals and audience engagement.

The platform helps users discover the best locations, considering factors like peak times for their followers, optimal lighting for photos, and local culture. With easy-to-follow itineraries and real-time insights, Wanderpath takes the guesswork out of planning, so content creators can focus on capturing content.

Key Takeaways

Prioritizing user needs and pain points helped shape features that enhance the experience of travel content discovery and planning.

Frequent testing and iteration allowed us to refine the platform based on real-time user feedback.

Cross-functional teamwork between design, development, and marketing ensured a well-rounded product.
